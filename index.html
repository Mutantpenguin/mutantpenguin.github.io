Universalis

My rules for a tabletop skirmisher.

Universalis tries to be a universal tabletop skirmisher for 28mm that emphasizes small games with few models. It is not tailored to a specific scenario and can be played in any universe.

This project is only in German.

Wasserschlacht

Rules for a children's tabletop game.

In Water Fight, you compete against 2 to 4 other players to see who is best at getting the others wet. You take turns moving your game pieces or getting the other game pieces wet with their water pistols or water bombs. Whoever is soaking wet has to go home and has lost!

This project is only in German.
